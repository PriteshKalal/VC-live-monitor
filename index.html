Standard Operating Procedure

1. Mandatory Booking Protocol

  • All VC requests must be formally raised through the Rajasthan Sampark portal. Manual requests via email or verbal communication will not be entertained for scheduling purposes.
  • Full details of all participating nodes (State HQ, Districts, or Blocks) and the number of attendees must be provided at the time of the Sampark request to ensure proper bookings.

2. Platform Usage & Link Distribution

  • To maintain security and resource priority, Cisco Webex links (created by RajVC Control Room) are strictly reserved for high-level meetings chaired by the Hon’ble Chief Minister (HCM) and the Chief Secretary (CS).
  • Meeting links for Hon’ble Chief Minister / Chief Secretary VCs shall be distributed exclusively by the Chief Minister’s Office (CMO) / CS Office team only.

3. Authorizations & Approvals

  • Any VC intended to involve District Collectors requires prior formal approval from the Chief Secretary. Evidence of this approval must be attached to the Sampark request.
  • Any modification to the VC timing (pre-ponement or post-ponement) must be formally confirmed by the concerned Department (AD) through mail before the technical team implements the change in the scheduler.

5. Slot Management & Conflict Resolution

  • VC slots are allotted on a first-come, first-served basis, subject to priority protocols.
  • In the event that a requested slot is unavailable due to
    • a prior booking or a high-priority state event, the initiating department is responsible for coordinating with the technical cell to identify and shift to an alternative suitable available slot, if available.
    • a prior booking, the requesting department/organization should coordinate with the concern department to mutually finalize rescheduling or modification of VC slots. RajVC team will not coordinate with concern department on behalf of requesting department / organization for rescheduling or modification of VC slots.
  • In case of Hon'ble CM Event, DoITC reserves the right to reschedule / cancel the scheduled VCs.

6. Technical Requirements & Presentation Guidelines

  • Departments must declare the requirement for PowerPoint (PPT) sharing at the time of booking.
  • If a presentation is required, the department must provide:
    • The name of the designated presenter.
    • The specific location/node from which the PPT will be shared.
  • For both Cisco and Webex platforms, the designated site must join the session 30 minutes prior for a technical dry run of the content sharing feature.

7. Technical Integrity & Remote Participation

  • For high-profile meetings, the use of soft links (joining via mobile/laptop) is strictly restricted to key participants who are traveling or cannot be physically present at a designated VC studio.
  • Requesting soft links/Webex access solely to avoid attending the office during weekends or holidays is strictly prohibited.
  • All District Level Officers (DLOs) and participants are expected to join from authorized VC rooms to ensure professional standards. Joining via soft links from uncontrolled environments is discouraged as it deteriorates VC quality due to:
    • Unstable personal bandwidth/network fluctuations
    • Substandard camera angles and poor lighting
    • Unprofessional backgrounds and ambient noise interference.

8. Important Email or IP phone numbers
